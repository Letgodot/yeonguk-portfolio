How I structure ambiguous work

  1. Observe where reality is messy.
  2. Identify hidden objects and rules.
  3. Define records, states, source traces, and outputs.
  4. Insert AI as an operating component.
  5. Keep review gates where judgment matters.

  • Context-preserving knowledge operations.
